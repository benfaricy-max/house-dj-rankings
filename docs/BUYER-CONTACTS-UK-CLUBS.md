## Systematic source (to go wider)

The canonical, always-current directory of who's promoting in each city is RA's
promoter index — each promoter page carries their own contact/booking route:

- Manchester promoters → [ra.co/promoters/uk/manchester](https://ra.co/promoters/uk/manchester)
- Leeds promoters → [ra.co/promoters/uk/leeds](https://ra.co/promoters/uk/leeds)
- London / Bristol equivalents by swapping the city slug.

To find *the exact buyer weighing a specific act*: open the act's RA page → look at
who's promoting their upcoming UK dates → that promoter is your named buyer. That's
the fastest path from "an act" to "a person with the budget."

## How to use this with the outreach pack

1. Pick an act + the buyer above who books it (e.g. **KETTAMA → Teletech**, **Josh
   Baker → Mint**, **PAWSA → Motion**).
2. Open the act's Value Gap report, copy a **Pitch Link**.
3. Paste the matching message from `OUTREACH-PRIORITY-A.md` — **DM first** (template
   C) for the underground brands, email where a real inbox exists (Mint, Motion).
4. Fill `[name]` with the promoter brand or the founder handle.
5. Close with the anchor ask. One reply → one call → one real fee.

> **Ethics note:** every contact here is a published business channel from an
> official site or RA. For any promoter without a public inbox, the correct route is
> their RA page or business IG — not a guessed address. Don't buy scraped
> "personal email" lists (the ContactOut/RocketReach results); they're low-quality
> and the wrong foot to start a trust-led pitch on.
