# Priority-1 CORA requests — ready to send

Five filled-in requests covering six roster acts (Chris Lake's settlement also carries Chris
Lorenzo's support guarantee). All are **executed past shows**, so a full settlement exists.

**Where to send:** Denver Arts & Venues CORA portal — https://www.artsandvenuesdenver.com/home/contact-us/cora-requests
(Red Rocks is owned by the City & County of Denver; artist performance agreements/settlements are
public records, including when held by the promoter.)

**Before sending:** add your name + contact as the requester. After a response lands, log the
guarantee in `docs/fee-anchor-tracker.csv`, convert USD→GBP at the show-date rate, and add the
anchor to `backend/fee_anchors.json` (`source: "contract"`). `computeFees.js` re-levels the whole
ladder once ≥3 anchors are in.
