# PEAKTIME — Sharpest Value Prop: Research Plan

## Background & Objectives
* **Context:** PEAKTIME (thedjrankings.com) is a multi-signal demand index for electronic-music artists. It carries several candidate value props — a composite ranking, **Momentum** (who's accelerating), **Value Gap** (underpriced vs. demand — the "buy signal"), **Reach vs. Credibility** (festival headliner vs. DJ's DJ), and a transparent **Scene Score**. The risk is offering all of them and landing none. Paywall is built but off; Pro is £49–99, Team £299–499.
* **The Big Q:** *Which single PEAKTIME signal or feature is the sharpest, most-trusted value proposition for each of bookers/promoters, artists/managers, and fans — the one that would change a real decision or behavior?*
* **Key Decisions this informs:**
  1. Which value prop leads the homepage and the pitch, per audience.
  2. Which signal to gate behind Pro vs. give away as the funnel hook.
  3. Which signal anchors the social content strategy (the @peaktime pillars).

## Design Summary
* **Method:** Semi-structured interviews with an embedded **reaction task** (participant navigates the live site / looks at 3–4 stimulus screens and thinks aloud). Value-prop questions are about mental models and *what would change a decision*, and the reaction task prevents purely aspirational answers.
* **Sample Size:** 6–8 per segment (18–24 total). Bookers and artists are the high-value, harder-to-recruit experts — 5 sharp ones beats 50 fans. Fans can sit at the lower end (5–6).
* **Session Duration:** 45–60 min (45 for fans, 60 for bookers/artists).
* **Timeline:** Recruit (1 wk) → interviews (1–2 wks) → synthesis (3–4 days). Compresses to days on Cookiy.

## Participant Profiles (behavior over demographics)
* **Bookers/Promoters:** Has personally booked ≥3 electronic acts in the last 12 months OR owns/programs a venue/festival/party series. Knows what a guarantee/fee is. *Exclude:* one-off wedding/corporate bookers with no scene context.
* **Artists/Management:** Actively gigging DJ/producer (≥6 paid shows last 12 mo) OR a manager/agent who reps ≥1 such artist. *Exclude:* hobbyists with no paid bookings.
* **Fans:** Attended ≥3 house/techno events in last 12 mo AND follows artists/scene media (RA, Beatport, Boiler Room, festival lineups). *Exclude:* casual radio-only listeners with no event behavior.
* **Across all:** exclude anyone working at a competing data/rankings product or market-research firm.

## Analysis Approach
* **Method:** Thematic analysis with In Vivo coding (preserve the trade's actual language — "guarantee," "buy signal," "DJ's DJ").
* **Synthesis:** A value-prop × segment matrix — for each segment, rank candidate signals by (a) spontaneous interest, (b) credibility/trust, (c) "would change my decision," and (d) willingness to pay.
* **Deliverable:** Per-segment "lead with this" recommendation + what to gate + the one trust objection to fix.
