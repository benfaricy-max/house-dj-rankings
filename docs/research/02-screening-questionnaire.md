# PEAKTIME — Screening Questionnaire

## Recruitment Goals
* **Primary Segments:** booker/promoter, artist/manager, engaged fan (route by Q1).
* **Target Count:** 6–8 per segment (18–24 total).
* **Incentive:** $60–80 / 45–60 min for bookers & artists (expert time); $25–40 for fans.

## Screening Questions

1. **[Routing/Exclusion — first]** In the last 12 months, which of these have you done for electronic/house/techno music? *(select all)*
   - Booked or paid an artist to play a venue/festival/party I run → **Booker segment**
   - Performed paid DJ/production gigs, or managed/agented an artist who does → **Artist segment**
   - Attended 3+ club nights or festivals as a fan → **Fan segment** (if no other box)
   - I work at a music-data, rankings, or market-research company → **Terminate**
   - None of these → **Terminate**

2. **[Behavioral depth — branched]**
   - *Bookers:* How many separate electronic acts have you personally booked in the last 12 months? (0 → Terminate · 1–2 → Continue, low priority · 3+ → Continue, priority)
   - *Artists/Mgmt:* How many paid shows in the last 12 months (you or your artist)? (0 → Terminate · 1–5 → Continue · 6+ → Continue, priority)
   - *Fans:* Which do you check at least monthly? Beatport / Resident Advisor / Boiler Room / festival lineups / DJ Mag / none. (None → Terminate)

3. **[Articulacy check — all]** Tell me about the last time you discovered an artist *before* they got big, or argued with someone about whether a DJ was over- or under-rated. What happened?
   - *Look for a specific, storied answer with names and detail. One-liners = poor fit for a qualitative interview.*
