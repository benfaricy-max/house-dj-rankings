Story angles

The most-streamed DJs aren't the most bookable

Streaming reach and booking demand barely correlate. Sven Väth pulls 23rd in booking demand on 104K monthly listeners. Some acts with 10M+ listeners sit past 300th. PEAKTIME is the first to quantify that gap across the whole field.

Which DJs are underpriced — and why it matters

A neutral fee benchmark reads booking demand against known fee bands. Jayda G currently reads about 3x under her live demand. The gap is model-implied; the demand signal is measured data. A tool promoters and agents can both cite from the same negotiation.

The ranking Spotify can't produce

Booking demand is built from live venue tier, touring density, DJ chart standing on Beatport, scene credibility, and what artists actually play in sets — not follower counts. The full method is public at thedjrankings.com.
